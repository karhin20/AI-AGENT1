Welcome to We Eating Good Kitchen, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of downtown. Our restaurant combines classic Ghanaian techniques with modern flavors to create a unique dining experience.

History:
Established in 2010 by renowned chef K Arhin, Eating Good Kitchen has quickly become a local favorite and a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts.

Menu:
Our menu changes seasonally to showcase the freshest local ingredients. We offer a variety of dishes, from our signature coq au vin to innovative vegetarian options like our butternut squash risotto. Our sommelier has curated an extensive wine list to perfectly complement each dish.

Dietary Accommodations:
We cater to various dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions.

Ambiance:
Our dining room features a blend of rustic charm and modern elegance, with exposed brick walls, soft lighting, and comfortable seating. The open kitchen allows diners to watch our skilled chefs in action.

Private Events:
We offer a private dining room that can accommodate up to 20 guests, perfect for intimate celebrations or business dinners. Our events team can help customize menus for your special occasion.

Sustainability:
We are committed to sustainable practices. We source our ingredients from local farms and fisheries, compost our food waste, and use energy-efficient appliances throughout our kitchen.
